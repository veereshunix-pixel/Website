Our Story

A Neighbourhood Favourite for Authentic South Indian Flavours

Sri Sagar Veg has been the heart of Jai Bheema Nagar, Seshadripuram for over a decade. Started with a simple dream — to serve wholesome, tasty, and affordable pure vegetarian food to working professionals and families — we've grown into a beloved local institution.

  • ✓Conveniently located near offices, schools, and residential areas in Seshadripuram
  • ✓Casual, clean, and comfortable dining space seating up to 60 guests
  • ✓Freshly prepared breakfast, lunch, and dinner — no stale or pre-cooked food served
  • ✓Catering to office group orders and bulk tiffin deliveries
  • ✓Friendly staff and quick service so you're never late to work
